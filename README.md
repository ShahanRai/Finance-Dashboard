# Finance Dashboard

A modern, beautiful, and privacy-friendly finance dashboard built with React, TypeScript, shadcn/ui, and Tailwind CSS.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Dashboard Overview**: Get a quick snapshot of your total balance, income, expenses, and credit usage.
- **Expense Breakdown**: Visualize and categorize your spending with interactive charts.
- **Income vs Expenses**: Track monthly trends and compare income to expenses.
- **Recent Transactions**: See your latest financial activities, color-coded for clarity.
- **Credit Card Management**:
  - Modern stacked card UI with real bank logos (auto-fetched)
  - Utilization badges, limits, and due dates
  - Add, edit, and delete cards easily
- **EMI Tracker**: Monitor active loans, monthly EMIs, and payment progress.
- **Investment Portfolio**: Track your investments and their performance.
- **Theme Toggle**: Switch between light and dark mode.
- **Fake Data Toggle**: Instantly preview the dashboard with demo data.
- **Currency Support**: Switch between USD and INR, with dynamic updates.
- **Responsive Design**: Works beautifully on desktop and mobile.

---

## 🛡️ Data Privacy

This app is designed for privacy. All your data stays on your device and is never sent to any server unless you connect your own backend.
